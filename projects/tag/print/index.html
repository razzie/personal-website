Magnetic Spinner

Download:
github.com/razzie/hobby-projects/2025/magnetic_spinner

Year: 2025

Tags: 3d print

Spin one of the rings and the other one will spin with it if you arrange the magnets right. Though it didn’t take considerable effort to design, it feels like a (useless) product and I like fidgeting with it, hence it received its own panel/description. The stand is removable to support modularity and potential future expansion. It was designed for 10x10x10mm magnet cubes, but consider scaling to 105% in your slicer before printing.

3D prints

Download:
github.com/razzie/hobby-projects/2022/3D_print_models

Year: 2022-2024

Tags: 3d print

I’ve been fascinated by magnets even since I was a kid, but it only turned into experimentation when I came across a video about Halbach arrays. I wondered whether I could come up with an unassisted levitation design, so I got myself an FLSUN Q5 delta 3D printer, a few dozens of magnetic cubes and learned how to create models in TinkerCad. Even though I did not succeed in my initial plan, I still ended up with some fun magnetic toys I can show my guests and a bunch of rock-paper-scissors dices for boardgames.
